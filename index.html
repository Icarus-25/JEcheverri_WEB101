About the Event

This is a vibrant community celebration featuring live performances, interactive activities, and delicious food. Designed for families, friends, and festival-goers of all ages, the event offers something for everyone—from music and dance to games and local vendors. Whether you're here to enjoy the entertainment, explore cultural showcases, or simply have fun, this event is your destination for unforgettable memories.

Event Schedule

Salsa Night

Get ready to move! Join us for an electrifying evening of salsa dancing, featuring live Latin music, vibrant rhythms, and expert-led dance sessions. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or just starting out, this night promises high energy and unforgettable fun.

Merengue Night

Feel the pulse of the Caribbean! Dive into a lively evening of merengue dancing with infectious beats, fast footwork, and joyful energy. Perfect for all skill levels—just bring your rhythm and let the music lead!

Bachata Night

Slow it down and feel the romance! Enjoy an intimate evening of bachata dancing with smooth steps, heartfelt melodies, and plenty of passion. Whether you're dancing close or just soaking in the vibe, it’s a night to connect and unwind.
